Publish DBA notice in local newspaper
California law requires publishing FBN in a local newspaper for 4 consecutive weeks. County clerk provides a list of qualifying papers. ~$50-100.

Confirm DBA does not need entertainment permits for established venue events
Hosting in a bar or established venue means the venue holds the permits. Confirm this with your attorney. DBA only needs to sign the venue rental agreement.
